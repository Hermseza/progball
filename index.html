Welcome to Progball!

Description:

Progball is a Souls-like bullet-hell dodging simulator.
The more balls you dodge, the more balls spawn and become faster.
Dodge more balls, score goes up.

Controls:

Press/click the Up/Down buttons on screen or Up/Down arrowkeys to move.
Mobile users can also press and hold anywhere on the screen (starting point) and move up/down from the starting point. Release to stop moving.
Don't get hit by the balls.
Press the Play button to start dodging.
Press the Modes button to spice things up.
And most importantly: have fun!

Progball Modes

Want to spice things up? Try a new mode!
With Shrink Mode enabled, your character will progressively get smaller.

Shrink Mode: Off

With the Enlarged ball mode, the balls will progressively get bigger.
With the Normal ball mode, the balls will stay the same size.
With the Shrunken ball mode, the balls will progressively get smaller.

Ball Mode: Normal
